Every Skylanders Trap Team Character! Complete Figure List

The fourth game in the Skylanders series, Trap Team again introduced several new figure types - most notably, the Trap Masters, and the Minis. At totally opposite ends of the spectrum, Minis are tiny, cute versions of popular Skylanders that pack all the same moves and health into a smaller package, while Trap Masters are huge figures that carry powerful traptanium weapons - perfectly suited to tackling the game's villains. Here's our complete list of all 80 Skylanders Trap Team figures - while below, you can also find a list of every character from the other games that's also compatible with Trap Team.
