Every Skylanders Imaginators Character! Complete Figure List

Skylanders Imaginators is the latest game in the Skylanders series, and promises to channel your imagination with its Imaginite Crystals - physical toys that let you create your own characters in game! Of course, that doesn't mean it'll be skimping on the proper figures either, as Imgainators will see the return of some familiar faces as Senseis. In story terms, these characters are there to help guide and train the Skylanders on their quest to defeat Kaos - but perhaps more excitingly, it gives us the chance to buy figures of our favourite villains for the first time. Promising around 30 Sensei characters, 20 of these are new, and 10 are reformed versions of Trap Team's villains, like Tae Kwon Crow, Golden Queen and Wolfgang.
